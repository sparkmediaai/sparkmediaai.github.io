Test 4: Usage Guidelines

Correct Usage

  • Use on dark backgrounds (#0C0F14)
  • Maintain minimum 20px clear space
  • Use SVG for web, PNG for fallback
  • Scale proportionally

Incorrect Usage

  • Do not alter colors
  • Do not stretch or distort
  • Do not add effects or shadows
  • Do not rotate or skew
  • Do not place on busy backgrounds
