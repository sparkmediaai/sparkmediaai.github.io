Services

Specialized Google Ads Management for Plumbing Companies

At SparkMedia.ai, we focus exclusively on helping plumbing businesses dominate Google search results. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the plumbing industry, from emergency services to routine maintenance.

Our Core Services

1. Strategic Google Ads Campaigns

  • Emergency Plumbing Keywords: Target customers searching for “emergency plumber near me”, “burst pipe repair”, “water leak emergency”

  • Geographic Targeting: Focus on your service areas with radius targeting and location extensions

2. Local SEO Optimization

  • Google Business Profile Management: Complete optimization of your GBP listing

  • Form Optimization: Reduce friction in lead capture forms
  • Trust Signals: Licenses, certifications, and customer testimonials

4. Performance Analytics & Reporting

  • Transparent Dashboard: Real-time access to campaign performance
  • Monthly Strategy Reviews: Deep dive into what’s working and optimization opportunities
  • ROI Tracking: Clear measurement of advertising spend to booked jobs
  • Competitive Analysis: Monitor competitor positioning and adjust strategy
